Weapons are implements capable of damaging Enemies. In Decaying Winter, weapons can be split into ranged and melee weapons, the former requiring ammunition. Weapons can be found across the map or crafted through blueprints, however weapons dropped by Enemies cannot be picked up.

There are currently 3 main categories of hostiles to both the players and other NPCS.

  • Ranged Weapons

  • Firearm/Guns

  • Melees
