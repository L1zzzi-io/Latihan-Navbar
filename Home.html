DECAYING WINTER

THE GAME

THE STORM IS COMING

AGENT

Decaying Winter is a hardcore wave-survival game released by Archeximus in September 2021. Agents are called in to defend the Safe Zone from AI enemies that are progressively getting more armed and dangerous, while also persevering through the storm. Once the storm has dissipated, Agents can scavenge for excess loot in order to prepare for the next inevitable storm and wave of enemies. The game itself has little to no handholding, but this is where you will find important information, tips and guides to survive.
