Enemies are hostile NPCS that serve as obstacles to prevent the player from attaining their goal. Majority of enemies will drop rations if killed during the looting phase of the night - they do not drop during the defense phase. Weapons dropped by enemies could not be picked up.

There are currently 3 main categories of hostiles to both the players and other NPCS.
