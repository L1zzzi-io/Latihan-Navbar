What are Perks?

Perks are classes that provide various buffs and cons, as well as a unique ability that sets it different against all other perks. They often times change a player's playstyle, and has an alignment that suggest the best way to play this perk. Do note that a perk's border classification is based on what the perk excels at with only its ability when alone without weapons, items or equipment. The purpose of a perk may not be as what it seems simply from its perk borders.
